Tier limits to enforce:

Free tier: 1 vehicle, 1 property, 1 family member, 0 receipt scans, no export, no cost analytics
Personal: 3 vehicles, 2 properties, unlimited family members, 15 scans/month, export enabled
Pro: 6 vehicles, 5 properties, unlimited family members, 30 scans/month, export + cost analytics
Business: unlimited everything, 100 scans/month

Critical rule: Never delete user data. Users who downgrade from trial to free keep all their vehicles, properties, and family members — they just cannot add new ones or access locked features. All existing data remains visible.

Before saving, query count of existing vehicles for this user

Use vehicleLimit(profile) from lib/subscription.ts — never hardcode the number

2. Property limit enforcement in app/add-property.tsx:

Same pattern using propertyLimit(profile)
Subtitle: 'Upgrade to add more properties'

3. Family member limit enforcement in app/add-family-member.tsx:

Free tier only: max 1 family member
Personal and above: unlimited
Subtitle: 'Upgrade to add unlimited family members'

When user taps the scan button:

If isFreeTier(profile): show <Paywall> with subtitle 'Upgrade to scan receipts with AI'
