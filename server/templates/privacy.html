Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: Email address and login details.

User-Provided Data: Vehicle, property, and maintenance data. Health-related scheduling information. Maintenance logs and records. Uploaded files (receipts, photos, documents). Audio recordings (for transcription features).

Device Information: Basic device information (device type, operating system) may be received by our infrastructure providers as part of normal service operation. We do not actively collect analytics, usage tracking, or performance data.

Derived Data: AI-generated schedules, insights, and estimates based on your inputs.

2. How We Use Information

We use your data to provide maintenance tracking and reminders, generate schedules, insights, and estimates, process receipts, images, and voice input, operate subscriptions and billing, improve product performance and reliability, and provide customer support. We do not sell your personal data.

3. AI Processing

Some features use third-party AI providers to process your data. This may include receipt scanning, voice transcription, and maintenance recommendations. Your data may be transmitted to these providers solely to perform these functions.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted service providers:

Supabase — data storage and infrastructure
RevenueCat — subscription management
Anthropic (Claude) — AI processing
OpenAI (Whisper) — transcription
Google Places API — location and address services
NHTSA API — vehicle data

These providers process only the data necessary to provide their services.

5. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored using Supabase infrastructure, encrypted in transit and at rest. We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data until you delete your account. Some data may be retained temporarily in backups or logs for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

7. Your Rights

You may access your data, update or correct certain information within the app (subject to system constraints designed to maintain data integrity — for example, mileage values may not be reduced once recorded), delete your account and associated data, and request data export where available.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance.
